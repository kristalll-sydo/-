# 雅思 9.0 及学术论文高阶词伙 (Expanded Edition)

抛弃简单的 "good", "bad", "important", 使用具有学术重量和精确度的“词伙 (Collocations)”。

## 一、 评价与影响 (Evaluation & Impact)

- **深远的影响**：exert a profound and enduring impact on... / leave an indelible mark on...
- **加剧问题**：exacerbate the underlying predicament / compound the existing dilemma
- **减轻负面效果**：mitigate the deleterious consequences of... / alleviate the adverse impacts
- **不可或缺的作用**：play an indispensable role in facilitating... / be of pivotal importance to...
- **产生适得其反的效果**：yield counterproductive outcomes / backfire spectacularly
- **带来了巨大的好处**：confer substantial benefits upon...
- **构成了严重威胁**：pose a formidable threat to...
- **彻底变革**：revolutionize the landscape of...

## 二、 逻辑关系表达 (Logical Relationships)

- **A 导致 B (高端表达)**：
  - A *gives rise to* B / A *is a catalyst for* B
  - A *triggers a chain reaction leading to* B
  - A *paves the way for* B
- **A 阻碍 B**：
  - A *acts as an impediment to* B / A *hinders the progression of* B
- **A 与 B 紧密相连**：
  - A is *inextricably intertwined with* B
  - There is a *symbiotic relationship between* A and B
  - A and B are *mutually constitutive*
- **强调重要性**：
  - It is *of paramount importance* to recognize that...
  - The significance of this phenomenon *cannot be overstated*.
  - This factor is *central to the discourse on*...

## 三、 描述趋势与变化 (Trends & Shifts)

- **急剧的模式转变**：a radical paradigm shift / a seismic shift in...
- **日益普及**：the burgeoning prevalence of... / the ubiquitous adoption of...
- **逐渐消退**：the gradual waning of... / the diminishing relevance of...
- **不可逆的趋势**：an irreversible trajectory toward...
- **历史性的高点/低点**：reach an unprecedented zenith / plummet to a historic nadir
- **长期的衰退**：a protracted period of stagnation

## 四、 表达确定性与不确定性 (Nuance & Hedging)

学术写作切忌绝对化，要熟练使用 Hedging（语气弱化）。
- **极有可能**：There is a strong probability that... / It is highly probable that...
- **似乎表明**：The empirical evidence strongly suggests that... / It appears indicative of...
- **某种程度上**：To a certain extent, / To a significant degree, ... / In some measure,
- **不可否认的是**：It is incontrovertible that... / It is indisputable that...
- **可以说**：It could be argued that... / Arguably, ...

## 五、 学术常用高分副词与连词 (Premium Adverbs & Connectors)

- **反常地 / 矛盾地**：*Paradoxically* (看似矛盾地是), *Ironically*
- **本质上**：*Inherently* (内在本质上地), *Intrinsically*
- **必然地**：*Invariably* (总是如此地 / 必然地), *Inevitably*
- **显著地**：*Conspicuously* (显著地), *Markedly*
- **成比例地**：*Proportionately* (成比例地)
- **相应地**：*Correspondingly*, *Accordingly*
- **相反地**：*Conversely* (用于对比相反情况)
- **尽管如此**：*Notwithstanding* (比 although 高级), *Albeit*

## 六、 针对数据的图表描述 (Data Description)

- **显著增长**：experience an exponential surge / witness a substantial upswing
- **趋于平稳**：plateau at a relatively stable level / level off
- **波动剧烈**：fluctuate wildly / exhibit profound volatility
- **成正/反比**：be directly/inversely proportional to...
- **占主导地位**：constitute the overwhelming majority / account for a lion's share
